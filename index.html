Hello there, I'm Jumee.
I’m a web developer, and a former graphic designer based in London, United Kingdom.

I’m a born creative who loves stories, whether they’re fictional or nonfictional. I worked for creative agencies in the London film industry for 8 years.

I love telling stories and taking on new challenges. As a millennial living in the 21st century, I have witnessed so many stories being shared and furthermore helping other people’s lives through technology. So I decided to be a part of the incredible phenomenon and enrolled a full-stack web development boot camp at General Assembly to learn new skills.

Learning to build an application and learning a new language have been a life-enriching process.

Having finished the Web Development Immersive course at GA, I’m now looking for a friendly and supportive team to create new and wonderful stories.

For a full career and education breakdown, please refer to my LinkedIn profile.

About

Portfolio and General Assembly

General Assembly is one of the biggest educational communities in tech, helping people to develop and realise their goals. Web Development Immersive is a 12-week full-time program, teaching the fundamentals of Web Development by industry experts. The course covers a range of programming languages and frameworks, both on the client side and server side.

As part of the course, I produced 4 projects, all of which used Agile working methodologies. The words 'Nature, Food, Art, and Life' represent the theme of my projects.

Please refer to my GitHub project pages for a full course curriculum.
